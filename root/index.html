 Blockchain

Here are a few projects that I'm following:

Polkadot

Polkadot is a blockchain interoperability protocol created by the Web3 Foundation. It allows disparate blockchains to exchange data trustlessly and can secure multiple chains simultaneously with pooled security. You can find out more about how it works here.

Loopring

Loopring is a DeFi protocol that leverages zk-rollups to increase transaction throughput on Ethereum by executing off-chain. With sharding on the horizon in Eth2, Loopring is closer to eliminating the high gas fees and low throughput plaguing many decentralized exchanges. You can find out more about how it works here.

MakerDAO

MakerDAO has created both a cryptocurrency (Dai) and a protocol which stabilizes the price of Dai. Unlike other stablecoins, Dai's price volatility is mitigated in a decentralized way. You can find out more about how it works here.

Uniswap

Uniswap is an automated market maker protocol on Ethereum. It acts as a decentralized exchange that facilitates trading between users without taking control of their assets. Some users provide liquidity in exchange for transcation fees while traders buy assets from their respective liquidity pools. Assets are priced using a constant product formula. You can find out more about how it works here.
